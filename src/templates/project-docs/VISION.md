# {{APP_TITLE}} Vision

## Vision Statement

{{APP_TITLE}} exists to make progress on [problem/mission] through reliable, iterative delivery powered by human judgment and AI-enabled execution.

## North Star

- Deliver measurable value quickly without sacrificing correctness.
- Keep the project understandable to both humans and AI contributors over time.
- Build systems that reduce rework through clear decisions, good defaults, and repeatable processes.

## Core Outcomes

- A product that is safe, usable, and maintainable.
- A working development loop with clear planning, review, and handoff signals.
- High confidence in releases through explicit verification and documented rationale.

## Boundaries

- We do not optimize for complexity; we optimize for clarity and maintainability.
- We avoid brittle one-off fixes that block future automation or reuse.
- We keep decisions local when possible and escalate only when impact crosses teams, data, or compliance boundaries.

## AI Collaboration Goals

- Use AI to accelerate research, coding, and documentation while keeping humans accountable for policy and final approval.
- Ensure every substantial AI-generated contribution is reviewable, attributable, and explainable.
- Preserve a continuous context trail (notes, decisions, and follow-up tasks) to support safe continuation.

## Success Indicators

- Fewer context gaps between sessions and contributors.
- Faster onboarding and handoffs with fewer missing decisions.
- Consistent delivery cadence with lower incident/rework rate.
